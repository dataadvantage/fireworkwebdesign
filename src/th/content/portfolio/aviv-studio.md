## AVIV Studio

AVIV Studio is a Bangkok-based interior design studio specializing in refined, contemporary spaces that balance aesthetics with functionality. Rooted in thoughtful design and attention to detail, the studio transforms residential and commercial environments into timeless interiors that reflect each client’s identity and lifestyle.

Based in Bangkok, Thailand, AVIV Studio works closely with homeowners, developers, and businesses to deliver tailored design solutions — from concept development and space planning to material selection and project coordination. Their approach combines modern sensibilities with warmth and craftsmanship, resulting in spaces that feel both elegant and livable.

With a strong focus on quality, proportion, and cohesive design language, AVIV Studio creates interiors that are not only visually striking but also practical and enduring.
