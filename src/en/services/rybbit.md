# Web Analytics with Rybbit

With **self-hosted Rybbit**, you get clear, useful analytics, while keeping full ownership of your data.

## What is Rybbit?

[Rybbit](https://rybbit.com/) is a privacy-focused website analytics tool.

It helps you see:

- How many visitors your website gets
- Which pages are being viewed
- Where traffic comes from
- How users move through your site

All without invasive tracking or selling user data.

## Why Self-Host Rybbit?

Self-hosting means the analytics system runs on **your own server**, not a third-party platform.

That gives you:

- **Full data ownership** - Your website data stays with you, not an external company.

- **Privacy-first analytics** - No ad tracking, no profiling, no unnecessary data collection.

- **Fewer compliance concerns** - A simpler setup for privacy-conscious businesses, especially in the EU.

- **Predictable costs** - No per-visitor fees or surprise pricing changes.

- **Independence & reliability** - You’re not affected by third-party outages or policy changes.

## Who Is This For?

This service is ideal if you:

- Care about user privacy and trust
- Want analytics without complex consent systems
- Prefer owning your data long-term

- Rybbit installation on your server
- Secure and privacy-friendly configuration
- Website integration

You get insights that are easy to understand and actually useful.
